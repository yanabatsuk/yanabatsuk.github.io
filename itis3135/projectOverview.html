Client Project

Project Overview

  1. Purpose of the Website: The website showcases the services and portfolio of professional photographer, Ethan Orange, specializing in various types of photography, including portraits, weddings, and engagements. It aims to increase visibility, attract new clients, and provide an easy way for potential customers to view his work and inquire about services. The site will present his skills and style while simplifying the booking process for prospective clients.
  2. Intended Users: Couples, families, and individuals looking for professional wedding, engagement, or portrait photography. The site will also be a valuable resource for other wedding and event professionals, such as planners and venues, looking to recommend a photographer with proven experience and a versatile portfolio.
  3. Website Content Overview: The website features a homepage with an overview of Ethan's services and welcome message, an about page highlighting his philosophy, a packages page detailing offerings, a gallery showcasing his work with external links, and a contact page for inquiries and bookings. Buttons will be included, where necessary, at the end of the pages to allow users to navigate more easily.
  1. Name of the Page: About Me
  2. Purpose of the Page: The About Me page serves to introduce the photographer, Ethan, to potential clients. It provides a personal insight into his background, philosophy, and what makes his photography services unique, establishing a personal connection with visitors.
  3. Audience/Users of the Page: Potential clients, individuals, couples, and families looking for a photographer who values artistry and authenticity in capturing special moments.
  4. Content of the Page:
    • A brief introduction to Ethan, including his background and passion for photography.
    • A section detailing Ethan's philosophy on photography, emphasizing artistic collaboration with clients.
    • A description of why clients should choose Ethan as their photographer, highlighting his creativity and dedication.
    • Images showcasing Ethan’s work or his personal brand, such as a profile photo or behind-the-scenes images.
    • Links to other key pages on the website (Gallery, Packages, and Contact) to encourage further engagement.
  5. Data Entry: No data entry is required on this page.
  6. Data Validation: Not applicable, as no user data is being entered.
  7. Interactive Elements:
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Navigate to the Gallery, Packages, and Contact pages.
  8. Actions on the Page:
    • Clicking on any of the navigation buttons (Gallery, Packages, or Book With Me) redirects users to the respective pages.
  9. Special Notes:
    • The page's tone is personal and professional, aligning with Ethan’s artistic approach to photography.
    • The section "Why Work With Me?" aims to build trust and emphasize the collaborative nature of Ethan’s work.
    • Ensure the page is visually engaging and uses appropriate imagery to complement the text, as this is the first personal introduction to Ethan’s work.

Page Design

  1. Name of the Page: Home Page
  2. Purpose of the Page: The home page serves as the entry point to the website, welcoming visitors and introducing them to Ethan's professional photography services. It provides a brief overview of the services offered and encourages users to explore other sections of the website.
  3. Audience/Users of the Page: Potential customers, including individuals, couples, and families looking for photography services.
  4. Content of the Page:
    • Welcome message and introductory text about the services provided.
    • A pop-up ad for promotions or special offers.
    • A slideshow showcasing sample images from Ethan’s portfolio.
    • Buttons with links to other key pages: Gallery, Packages, and Contact.
  5. Data Entry: No data entry is required on this page.
  6. Data Validation: Not applicable, as no user data is being entered.
  7. Interactive Elements:
    • Pop-up Ad Close Button: Closes the pop-up ad.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Navigate to the Gallery, Packages, and Contact pages.
    • Slideshow Navigation: Automatically cycles through images; no manual interaction needed.
  8. Actions on the Page:
    • When users click on the pop-up ad close button, the ad closes without further action.
    • Clicking on navigation buttons redirects users to corresponding pages.
  9. Special Notes:
    • The page includes a pop-up that can highlight ongoing promotions, requiring an effective design to ensure it is visually appealing without being intrusive.
    • The slideshow enhances the page's visual appeal, showcasing Ethan's photography style right from the start.
    • Ensure the page is responsive and loads efficiently across devices to maintain a smooth user experience.
