Client Project

Project Overview

  1. Purpose of the Website: The website showcases the services and portfolio of professional photographer, Ethan Orange, specializing in various types of photography, including portraits, weddings, and engagements. It aims to increase visibility, attract new clients, and provide an easy way for potential customers to view his work and inquire about services. The site will present his skills and style while simplifying the booking process for prospective clients.
  2. Intended Users: Couples, families, and individuals looking for professional wedding, engagement, or portrait photography. The site will also be a valuable resource for other wedding and event professionals, such as planners and venues, looking to recommend a photographer with proven experience and a versatile portfolio.
  3. Website Content Overview: The website features a homepage with an overview of Ethan's services and welcome message, an about page highlighting his philosophy, a packages page detailing offerings, a gallery showcasing his work with external links, and a contact page for inquiries and bookings. Buttons will be included, where necessary, at the end of the pages to allow users to navigate more easily.
